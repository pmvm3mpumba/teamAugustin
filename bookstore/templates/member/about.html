About This Project

This project, BookApp, is a Django-based online library system developed on the occasion of a Python programming exam by the students Jean-Pierre, Augustin, and Daniel. The main purpose of BookApp is to connect people who have personal home-based libraries into a shared digital library. Users can upload books they wish to share and access books shared by others from different countries across the globe.
